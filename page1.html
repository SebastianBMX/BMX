Page 1; Types of BMX

Park: Lightweight without any modifications.
Dirt: Wider tires for more grip and larger frames.

Flatland: Balanced bikes for performing complex tricks and routines.

Race: Better brakes, with a larger sprocket for higher speeds.

Street: Heavier frames with pegs on the wheels, they often have no brakes.
